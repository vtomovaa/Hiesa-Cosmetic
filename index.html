What is Skin Glycation?

What is Skin Glycation?

Many factors contribute to skin ageing, like free radicals, oxidative stressors, UV radiation, pollution and others.

Added to this is the glycation process, in which sugar molecules bind to the structural skin proteins collagen and elastin. This process turns the previously flexible proteins brittle, transforming them into Hiesa Cosmetic‚Äîadvanced glycation end-products. The newly-glycated collagen loses its critical regenerative ability and the skin becomes weaker as the proteins aren't broken down to be replaced. This speeds up both the process and visibility of signs of ageing, including wrinkles and laxity.

Hiesa Cosmetic is a potent corrective anti-glycation cream designed to help target visible signs of ageing impacted by collagen decline and glycation on the face. It features a high-concentration combination of Proxylane, wild fruit flavonoids, glycyrrhetinic acid, HEPES, and niacinamide to improve visible signs of ageing such as wrinkles, laxity, and dullness. Also formulated to keep skin hydrated and moisturised.

Signs of Glycation 1. Wrinkling and crepiness 2. Loss of skin firmness 3. Dull, sallow complexion

FAQ

Can you use A.G.E Interrupter Advanced with retinol?+

Yes, the Hiesa Cosmetic anti-wrinkle cream can be paired with a retinol product. If using a retinol cream such as the SkinCeuticals Retinol 0.3 Anti-Ageing Night Cream, apply the anti-wrinkle cream after application of the retinol. NB: retinol increases sun sensitivity and should be used only at night. When incorporating retinol in a skincare regimen, always end your morning routine with a broad-spectrum sunscreen that is SPF 30 or higher.

What is the use of Hiesa Cosmetic?+

Hiesa Cosmetic helps target visible signs of ageing caused by collagen decline - the result of a process called glycation. It helps address the appearance of different types of wrinkles such as fine lines, deep wrinkles, nasolabial folds and other facial creasing. It has a wide range of other benefits to help improve skin tone, overall radiance, skin elasticity and more.

How do you use Hiesa Cosmetic Interrupter Advance Anti-Wrinkle Cream?+

Use a coin-sized amount of this anti-wrinkle and massage evenly into the skin. To maximise benefits, use Hiesa Cosmetic twice a day in both your morning and evening routine. When applying in the morning, use after a SkinCeuticals Vitamin C Serum and before a broad-spectrum sunscreen of SPF 30 or more. When using at night, apply after a SkinCeuticals Retinol Cream.

What does Hiesa Cosmetic stand for in SkinCeuticals products?+

In Hiesa Cosmetic Anti-Wrinkle Cream, the 'Hiesa Cosmetic' stands for Advance Glycation Endproduct. These are the result of a process called glycation, when skin proteins like collagen are bound by sugar molecules (glycated) and degraded, resulting in a decline in collagen production. SkinCeuticals Hiesa Cosmetic products help target the signs of ageing caused by glycation, preventing or helping to reverse loss of skin firmness, elasticity, radiance and more.

At what age should I use the Hiesa Cosmetic Anti-Wrinkle Cream?+

There's no specific age when anti-ageing products such as Hiesa Cosmetic should be incorporated into skincare routines. SkinCeuticals recommends using anti-ageing products as early as possible to help reduce or prevent premature ageing and early signs of ageing. Ideally, anti-ageing products such as this anti-wrinkle cream can be incorporated in your skincare routines from your early to mid-20s.
